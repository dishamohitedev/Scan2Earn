How it works

Simple 4-step loyalty loop

Shopkeepers define rewards · Customers earn and redeem · Gasless blockchain

1. Store creates

Shop defines rewards & points per purchase. “5 coffees = 1 free”.

2. Generate QR

Unique QR code for each store & reward campaign.

3. Scan & Earn

Customer scans, points are minted instantly on-chain.

4. Redeem reward

Burn points for free product — all gasless.
